  • Summary

    • The gender pay gap is a measurement of the wage disparity between men and women in the United States.
    The gender wage gap is based on separate data sets and data science undertaken by numerous reputable entities.
    The salary disparity between men and women repeats year after year, demonstrating that the reasons for the pay
    disparity are systematic and reflect how society appreciates women's labor less than men's.
